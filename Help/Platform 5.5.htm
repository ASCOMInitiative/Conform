Platform 5.5 Update

Platform 5.5 introduces additional exceptions that drivers can use, notably InvalidValueException, NotConnectedException, ParkedException and ValueNotSetException. These do not mandate the use of error numbers, relying instead on the type of exception to indicate the nature of the issue.

Over time the value of error numbers is planned to diminish in favour of an increased number of well defined exception types, however, the abliity to specify device specific test commands is expected to remain useful in the long term.
